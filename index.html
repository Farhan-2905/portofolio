Teaching Methodology

Project-Based Learning

Students learn by working on real-world projects with actual datasets, developing practical skills that translate directly to industry needs.

Flipped Classroom

Theory is covered through pre-recorded lectures and readings, while class time is dedicated to hands-on exercises, discussions, and problem-solving.

Industry Collaboration

Regular involvement of industry partners to provide students with exposure to current challenges and opportunities in the field.

Personalized Mentoring

Tailored guidance for each student based on their background, learning style, and career goals.

Cross-Disciplinary Approach

Integrating knowledge from various domains to solve complex problems, reflecting the multidisciplinary nature of data science.

Continuous Assessment

Regular feedback through code reviews, project milestones, and peer evaluations to ensure continuous improvement.
